You don't have to hand-build the storefront that sits on top of this API. **Anvil** turns a
plain-language description of the store you want into a complete, deployed storefront — already
wired to your Galactic Core catalog, cart, checkout, payments, search, and the rest of the SDK.

## Why it fits Galactic Core

Everything documented here — the [TypeScript SDK](/sdk/introduction), the service surface, the
conventions in [Core Concepts](/concepts) — is the same surface Anvil generates against. So a
storefront it produces uses the real methods (`client.products.listProducts`, `client.orders`,
`client.payments`, `client.search`, recommendations, analytics, currency from store info, and so
on) the same way you would by hand — just generated for you.

## When to reach for it

- You want a storefront live quickly without writing the integration plumbing first.
- You're prototyping a concept and want a real, data-backed store to react to.
- You're a partner who wants to offer customers an AI-generated storefront on top of their own
  Galactic Core backend.

Prefer to build by hand? That's fully supported — start with the [Quickstart](/quickstart) and the
[TypeScript SDK](/sdk/introduction). Anvil is simply the fastest path from idea to a running
storefront on the same foundation.
